Different languages and cultures have different views on what makes an essay good or correct. Here are some of the differences and what is important in English writing:

        1. State the opinion (thesis statement) in the first paragraph, the introduction. In some languages this is not stated until the end.
        
        2. Avoid phrases like "I think" or "I believe" as it is understood that everything stated is either a fact or opinion.
